COOL RANCH

What's better than a nice hot plate of wings? A nice hot plate of Ranch wings! A varient of a simple wing recipe, this version is packed full of cool ranch flavor.

INGREDIENTS

  • 12 whole chicken wings
  • hidden valley ranch
  • water
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • sugar
  • paprika
  • mayo
  • buttermilk
  • sour cream
  • green onions

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Use a boning knife to separate your chicken wings into drums and flats.

  2. Whisk together ranch, water, salt, and pepper, and allow the chicken wings to soak in that mixture for at least 2 hours, or overnight.

  3. Drain the chicken and pat dry on paper towels.

  4. Heat oil to 350 degrees. Combine All seasonings in a small bowl.

  5. Fry wings in batches of 8 for 8 minutes each. Remove with a slotted spoon and allow to drain on paper towels. Set the cooked wings on a baking sheet in the oven on warm setting while the rest cook.

  6. Once all the wings are cooked, add to a mixing bowl, sprinkle with ranch seasoning, and serve.

  7. Add mayo, buttermilk, sour cream, vinegar, worcestershire sauce, black pepper, and salt to a medium mixing bowl and whisk together.

  8. Finely chop green onion and parsley and add it to the bowl. Finely grate garlic, add it to the bowl, and whisk. Serve wings with ranch.
