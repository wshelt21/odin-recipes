PEACH HABANERO

The habanero peach shares a similar heat profile to most other habanero peppers, though its floor tends to be a little hotter. Because of this, these wings can really pack a punch if you you're looking for something spicy but also a sweet tangy flavor!

INGREDIENTS

  • chicken wings
  • boar's night out spicy white lightning all purpose rub
  • heavy smoke black label rub
  • peachy peach habanero rib candy

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Start by trimming your wings

  2. Put down a layer of Spicy White Lightning and Heavy Smoke.

  3. Once you season the wings, go start your grill.

  4. Once they are almost done, start glazing your wings with the Rib Candy.The wings are done when they reach 190-200 degrees internal temperature.
